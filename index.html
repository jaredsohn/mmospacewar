What is this?

  • A modification of MMO Asteroids (an actual MMO Asteroids without the April Fools' joke, made possible thanks to a super awesome API) to make the rules more like the DOS version of Spacewar. We stand on the shoulders of giants...
  • You can now warp (by pressing 'z').
  • You don't die right after getting shot. Instead you have energy (shown in the upper right next to your score.)
  • It costs energy to use thrust, warp, or if you collide with a bullet or other ships. You gain back energy slowly over time.
  • Your opponents' ships become more red as their energy decreases.
  • Once you die, you're done (and can see your score in the scoreboard).
  • At the moment, you are playing in the same world as the MMO Asteroids people and are sharing their scoreboard. Be nice.
